# 📊 Profit Optimization Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes transaction-level data to identify key drivers of profitability and evaluate strategies to improve profit.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify key drivers of profitability
- Evaluate impact of discount and cost on margin
- Test strategies through simulation
- Provide business recommendations

---

## 📊 Key Findings
- Profit is not driven by category or region
- Discount has the strongest impact on margin
- Cost contributes but with smaller effect
- Discount optimization alone is not sufficient

---

## 🧪 Simulation Results

| Scenario | Strategy | Impact |
|----------|----------|--------|
| Sim 1 | Cap Discount ≤ 20% | +2.47% |
| Sim 2 | Reduce High Discount | +0.42% |
| Sim 3 | Target Low Margin | -0.95% |
| Sim 4 | High Discount + High Cost | -0.01% |

---

## 💡 Insights
Profitability is driven by transaction-level efficiency rather than segmentation factors.

---

## 🚀 Recommendation
- Reduce high discount gradually
- Improve cost efficiency
- Focus on transaction-level optimization

---

- Python (Pandas, Seaborn, Statsmodels)
